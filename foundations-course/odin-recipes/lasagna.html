Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layered pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Each layer is baked together until warm, savory, and perfectly comforting. It’s a hearty meal often enjoyed during family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Onion and garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs (oregano, basil)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, add ground meat, cook until browned, then mix in tomato sauce and seasonings.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese (and egg if using) with a bit of salt and pepper.
  4. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and cheeses—repeat until full.
  5. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan, then bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 40–45 minutes until bubbly.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
